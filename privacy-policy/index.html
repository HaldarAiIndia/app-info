"Personal Data" means any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.

"Usage Data" means data collected automatically about how the App and your device are used.

2 Data We Collect

To deliver its core functionality, the App requests the following Android permissions:

  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES
    Allows the App to read a list of all installed applications on your device, including package names, version codes, and app metadata.
  • GET_PACKAGE_SIZE
    Allows the App to read the storage size (cache, data, code) of installed applications.
  • PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS
    Allows the App to access usage statistics — how often and how long each app is used. This requires explicit user approval via Android system settings.
  • INTERNET
    Allows the App to connect to the internet for updates, analytics, or other online features.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS
    Allows the App to send you notifications about App activity or updates.
Note: All data accessed through these permissions is used solely on-device to display information to you. We do not transmit your installed app list or usage statistics to any external server unless explicitly stated.
